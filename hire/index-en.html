Hire an intern.

Looking for trainee web developers between October 30 and December 22, 2017?

Who are they?

The BeCode Learners

BeCode BeCode helps highly motivated and independent people become junior web developers within 6 months. The training is very intensive since 6 months is a very short period. They are making a turning point in their career. Our coders are excited and have learnt to learn quickly. You can help them achieve a successful retraining ; they can help you deal with your workflow and achieve your goals. After an intense 6-month training, our learners are ready to dive into your professional code.

What they can offer you.

They have been using github from day one. They have learnt (pedagogy based on practical projects) markdown, terminal, HTML, CSS, Javascript, PHP, MySQL, but most of all, they have learnt to learn, to find their way with Google, to always try and find a solution for their team.

For them, the internship is the opportunity to get a first experience as a coder, activating their new technical and soft skills. It also allows them to extend their learning by confronting what they have learnt at BeCode with the reality of business.

The tasks, missions and activities you will assign them will be linked to :

  • Front-end development: HTML, CSS, Javascript
  • Back-end development: PHP, MySQL
  • Working prototypes development
  • Upkeeping existing code
  • Learning new languages

What they can’t do

Coffee and copies.

What we expect from the company

The company is committed to providing a reference person (training supervisor) who will coach the learner during the internship, making sure the trainee’s integration within the company is going fine and giving them work that is linked with the aforesaid goals.

When? How long?

The internship takes place between october 30 and December 22, 2017. It can be extended to reach a maximum period of 3 months.

BeCode wants to challenge established standards in the training field by offering a continuous cycle: Every three months, we will offer new web development trainees the opportunity to take the plunge into the professional world by doing an internship within a company.
